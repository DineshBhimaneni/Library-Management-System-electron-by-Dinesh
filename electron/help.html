Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a book?
Visit the Books Catalog, find your book, and click "Borrow" if available.
How do I pay a fine?
Go to Fines & Payments, select the fine, and click "Pay Now."
How do I renew a book?
Go to My Books and click "Renew" on the book you want to extend.
Where can I find e-books?
All digital books are available in the E-Books section.
