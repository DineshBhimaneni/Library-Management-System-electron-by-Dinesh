Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a book that's not available in the library?

You can request books through our interlibrary loan service. Navigate to the "New Request" section in the portal, fill out the form with the book details, and our staff will process your request within 2-3 business days.

What is the loan period for faculty members?

Faculty members enjoy a 90-day loan period for most books, with up to 2 renewals if there are no pending requests for the same item. Journals and special collections may have shorter loan periods.

How can I access electronic resources off-campus?

Use the university VPN or proxy server to access electronic resources remotely. Your faculty credentials will authenticate you. Detailed setup instructions are available in the "Electronic Resources" guide.

Can I recommend new acquisitions for the library?

Yes! We welcome faculty recommendations. Use the "Recommend a Purchase" form in the portal or contact your department's library liaison directly.

How do I set up course reserves for my students?

Visit the "Course Reserves" section in the portal or contact the reserves desk at least 2 weeks before the start of term. You'll need to provide course information and materials list.
